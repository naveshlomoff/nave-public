BruxTrack iOS

Installation guide · iPhone · Safari

iOS is different from Android. Safari does not prompt you to install — you must add to Home Screen manually. Notifications require iOS 16.4 or later. Background audio requires the screen to stay on or Guided Access to be enabled.

Step 1 — Install from Safari
1

Open in Safari (not Chrome)

BruxTrack must be opened in Safari — PWA installation only works from Safari on iOS. Chrome, Firefox, and other browsers on iPhone use WebKit but cannot install PWAs.

2

Tap the Share button

Tap the Share button (square with arrow pointing up) in the Safari toolbar at the bottom of the screen.

3

Tap "Add to Home Screen"

Scroll down in the Share sheet and tap Add to Home Screen. You can rename it — keep "BruxTrack".

4

Tap Add

Tap Add in the top-right corner. The app icon appears on your Home Screen. Open it from there — this is what activates standalone PWA mode.

Step 2 — Allow microphone
A

Tap the unlock screen

When you open BruxTrack, you'll see a startup screen. Tap "Open BruxTrack" — this is required on iOS because Safari needs a direct user tap to activate the audio engine.

B

Allow microphone when prompted

Tap the record circle — iOS will ask for microphone permission. Tap Allow. If you accidentally denied it, go to Settings → BruxTrack → Microphone → Allow.

Step 3 — Keep it recording all night
A

Plug in to charge

Recording audio all night uses battery. Keep your iPhone plugged in. This also prevents Low Power Mode from activating and throttling the processor.

B

Enable Guided Access

Go to Settings → Accessibility → Guided Access and turn it on. Then triple-click the side button while in BruxTrack to lock the app in the foreground. This prevents iOS from suspending audio when the screen dims.

C

Increase screen timeout or keep screen on

Go to Settings → Display & Brightness → Auto-Lock and set to Never while recording. Remember to restore it afterwards.

D

Place the phone correctly

Face-down on the mattress 15–30 cm from your head — picks up jaw vibration through the mattress. This is the primary and most sensitive placement.

Notifications (iOS 16.4+ only)
!

Notifications require the installed PWA

Web push notifications on iOS only work when the app is installed via Add to Home Screen and you are on iOS 16.4 or later. Open the installed app, go to Settings, and tap "Enable Notifications" once you've completed the install steps.
